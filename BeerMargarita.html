INGREDIENTS IN THE GINGER BEER MARGARITA COCKTAIL

10 oz Casa Noble Crystal tequila

Garnish: Lime wheel Glass: Highball


HOW TO MAKE THE GINGER BEER MARGARITA COCKTAIL

In a small bowl, stir together the agave nectar and lime juice until the agave nectar dissolves. Add to a pitcher, along with the tequila and ginger beer, and stir well. Add 2 cups ice and stir until cold. Serve in highball glass. Garnish with a lime wheel.
